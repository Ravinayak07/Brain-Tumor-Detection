Binary Classification

SYMPTOMS

1) Headache: can be persistent or severe
2) Muscular: difficulty in walking
3) Cognitive: inability to speak
4) Gastrointestinal: nausea or vomiting
5) Whole body: dizziness, fatigue, or vertigo
6) Sensory: pins and needles or reduced sensation of touch

TREATMENT

1) Surgery
2) Chemotherapy
3) Clinical trials
4) Radiation therapy
5) Targeted drug therapy
6) Tumor treating fields
7) Follow-up care and rehabilitation
